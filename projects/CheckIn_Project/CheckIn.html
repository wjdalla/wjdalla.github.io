Check In - Brand Creation and Website

The project I came up with for the final assignment in my Professional Technical Course in Graphic Design (year 12) was called Check In. For this project, I had to create a brand and an identity for it. At the time, I developed two main projects for the association and created a website, merchandise, posters and social media content. Four years later, I decided to develop a website from scratch to replace the original template-based website.

Solo Project: Daniela Teodoro

Methodology: Topic Research | Visual Identity | Logo Development | Merchandise | Wireframes | Website Prototype

Concept

The logo is made up of a ‘C’ and an ‘I’ that circumscribes the African and American continents. The ‘C’ and “I”, which represent
the initials of the brand's name, seem to ‘revolve around the continents’, representing the brand's expansion and presence in the world, as well as the wide range of sustainable choices that Check in offers.

Naming

The name ‘Check In’ was chosen to represent the main ideas of the brand's projects, referring to sustainable products and also fitting in with sustainable tourism, because of airport check-ins.

Colors

The brand's main colours are blue and green, which
represent what we can do for planet earth in the future. The blues reflect the future and customers' trust in the brand, also representing the oceans, and the green represents the colour of sustainability and the green of vegetation on a healthy planet.
